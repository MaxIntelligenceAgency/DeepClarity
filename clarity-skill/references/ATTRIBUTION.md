## What was copied, and what wasn't

- **Not copied:** the 29k content corpus is not reproduced in this skill. Exercise scripts, slide text, lottie/audio/video assets, and per-item content live only in `29k-source/content/`.
- **Distilled, not copied:** the *patterns* documented in `notes/patterns.md` — the 6-phase exercise arc, the invitational voice register, the three-sentence sharing rules, the "no-right-or-wrong" reflection framing — shape `SKILL.md` but are expressed in Clarity's own words.
- **Quoted verbatim:** only the short excerpts under this directory (`excerpt-01` … `excerpt-05`), each marked with its source file path, slide type, and the exercise it came from. The sharing-rule excerpt in particular is reproduced in full because it is the philosophical anchor of Clarity's scope.
